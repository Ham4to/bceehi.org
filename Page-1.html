About Us

Bilkis Child Education Empowerment and Humanitarian Initiatives is an organization dedicated to providing education and empowering children in need. Through their various programs and initiatives, they aim to ensure that every child has access to quality education and opportunities for personal development. The organization also focuses on humanitarian efforts, providing assistance to those in need and advocating for the rights of vulnerable populations. Their work has made a meaningful impact on the lives of many individuals and communities, and they continue to strive for a brighter, more equitable future for all.

BCEEHIBilkis Child Education Empowerment & Humanitarian Initiatives

BCEEHI Africa is a non-governmental and non-profit making organization in Ghana registered under the Companies Act, 2019, (Act 992). BCEEHI Africa was founded by Ms. Barikisu Suleiman, 2023 Ghana’s National Best Primary Teacher.  Ms. Suleiman is not only a dedicated educator but also the Curriculum Lead at Answaru-Deen Islamic Basic School in New Fadama, Accra, Ghana. She serves on the District Teacher Support Team for the Okaikwei North Municipal Education Directorate in Ghana. Through her years of service, Ms. Suleiman has witnessed firsthand the struggles faced by orphans and children from low-income backgrounds. Inspired by these challenges, she established BCEEHI Africa to provide these vulnerable children with critical educational resources, hygiene products, and scholarships. BCEEHI Africa aims to bridge the gap between the academic needs of disadvantaged children and the resources available to them, empowering them to access opportunities that can significantly improve their futures.

Vision

To be at the forefront of educational empowerment and humanitarian support, creating a lasting and sustainable impact across Africa.

Values

Our core values are integrity, respect, inclusivity, equity, excellence, compassion, innovation, and sustainability.

Mission

We strive to transform education and improve the lives of marginalized communities throughout the continent. Our dedication lies in promoting environmental stewardship and empowering learners to drive positive change, particularly in areas such as climate change. Through holistic support and empowerment, our aim is to build a resilient and sustainable Africa, where education and humanitarian efforts are the foundations of lasting transformation and progress

A BEAM OF HOPE PROJECT 

is an initiative of BCEEHI Africa designed to tackle the pressing challenges that prevent children from deprived backgrounds in Ghana from accessing quality education. The project focuses on Answaru-Deen Islamic Basic School in New Fadama, Accra, Ghana, as its starting point, with plans to expand to other deprived communities in the country.
